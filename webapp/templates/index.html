How It Works

From document to intelligent conversation

RAGify follows the Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline to deliver accurate, hallucination-free answers.

01

Upload Documents

Drag and drop your PDF files into the platform. RAGify accepts single or multiple documents at once.

📥
02

Split & Embed

Documents are intelligently chunked and converted into vector embeddings using state-of-the-art transformer models.

🧠
03

Store in Vector DB

Embeddings are stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL + pgvector) for persistent, scalable semantic search.

🗄️
04

Ask & Retrieve

When you ask a question, the system finds the most relevant chunks from your documents using semantic similarity.

🔍
05

Generate Answer

The LLM generates a precise, context-grounded answer based on the retrieved passages — with source citations.
